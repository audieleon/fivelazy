Last updated: July 7, 2026

Five Lazy LLC makes games for Apple platforms. Our games don't collect your personal information. This page explains what that means.

What we collect

Nothing. Our games run entirely on your device. Your progress, settings, and saves stay in the app's local storage on your device and are never sent to us or anyone else. We don't collect your name, email, contacts, location, or device identifiers, and we don't run servers that receive your data.

Apple Game Center

If you sign in to Apple Game Center, your leaderboard scores and achievements are handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy. That's your choice, the game works fine without it, and we never see your Game Center account.

Purchases

If a game sells optional content, Apple handles the purchase through the App Store. We're told a purchase happened. We never see your name, card, or billing details.

Ads and analytics

We don't use them. No ad networks, no tracking, no third-party analytics or profiling SDKs. We don't sell or share your personal information.

Children

Our games collect no personal information from anyone, so they collect none from children.

Changes

If a future game or feature changes how data is handled, we'll update this page and the date above before it ships.
